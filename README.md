# AEGYON — Partner Kit for Consultants & Advisors

This repository is for consultants, advisory firms, and strategy practices who advise companies and want to embed structural diagnostics into their client offerings.

We sit invisibly behind you as the engine. You own the client relationship and the brand on the cover. Your client pays you; we share 50/50. You keep half of every engagement.

## Open this in a browser

Start at **`index.html`** — it is the visitor entry point. Everything else is reachable from there.

## The partner model in one paragraph

You quote one of three engagement tiers to your client. We deliver the structural dossier and supporting collaterals in your name within 5–7 business days. You collect from your client. You keep 50%. We collect 50% on Net 15. Optional quarterly continuity retainer keeps the relationship live and turns Year 2+ into largely pure margin.

## Pricing tiers (suggested retail)

- Tier 1 — Rapid Assessment: $2K–$7K, 3–5 business days.
- Tier 2 — Comprehensive Analysis: $8K–$20K, 5–7 business days.
- Tier 3 — Critical Validation: $20K–$50K, 2–3 days expedited.
- Continuity Retainer (optional): $5K–$10K/quarter, mostly margin after Year 1.
